With the given data of various crimes for 59 years linear regression model is determined and the crime for 9 years ahead is predicted. The system is trained by feeding previous fifty nine years of data taken from National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), Delhi, India statistics (1953-2012). Crime prediction is done for total cognizable crimes, murder, kidnapping and abduction, dacoity, robbery, burglary, theft, riot and other such crimes. This work will be helpful for the local police stations in decision making and crime suppression. Keywords: Data mining, Crime prediction, regression, linear regression, R Programming.
